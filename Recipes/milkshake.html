Mamaw's homemade recipe of cheesy goodness will surely bring the smiles to your next potluck. Easy to follow, no-frills recipe using just cheddar cheese.

Ingredients

    4 large scoops (1 1/2 cups) of your favorite icecream 1/4 cup milk Whipped cream for garnish Sprinkles for garnish Maraschino cherry for garnish

Instructions

    In a blender, add icecream and milk, blend together Garnish with whipped cream, sprinkles, and a maraschina cherry
